Compounding

Imagine I am your employer, and I have an offer for you. I will give you an amount which you must wait 30 years to touch.

I can start your pot with £0.01, and it will double every year. Or, I can start your pot with £10,000, and I will add a further £10,000 each year.

Which one do you pick?

It is now year two.

You chose to take the penny. You have £0.02 now. If you had taken the £10,000 you would now have £20,000.

You chose to take the £10,000. You have £20,000. If you had taken the penny you would now have £0.02.

Remember, there are still 29 years left for your pot to grow.

It's not too late to change your mind! I have set up both pots in case you want to. Do you want to switch?

It is now year five.

You chose to take the penny. You have £0.16 now. If you had taken the £10,000 you would now have £50,000.

You chose to take the £10,000. You have £50,000. If you had taken the penny you would now have £0.16.

Remember, there are still 25 years left for your pot to grow.

Do you want to switch now?

It is now year ten.

You chose to take the penny. You have £5.12 now. If you had taken the £10,000 you would now have £100,000.

You chose to take the £10,000. You have £100,000. If you had taken the penny you would now have £5.12.

Remember, there are still 20 years left for your pot to grow.

Do you want to switch now?

It is now year twenty.

You chose to take the penny. You have £5,242.88 now. If you had taken the £10,000 you would now have £200,000.

You chose to take the £10,000. You have £200,000. If you had taken the penny you would now have £5,242.88.

Remember, there are still 10 years left for your pot to grow.

Do you want to switch now?
